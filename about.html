Our Story

Travel should be about exploration, not finding a SIM card shop.

NoorLink began with a simple frustration: landing in a new country and being disconnected. The expensive roaming fees, the hunt for local Wi-Fi, and the hassle of swapping physical plastic SIM cards felt outdated.

We built NoorLink to solve this. By leveraging eSIM technology, we connect travelers to local networks digitally. No shipping, no plastic waste, and no waiting. Just instant connection the moment you land.
