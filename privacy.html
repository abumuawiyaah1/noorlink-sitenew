Privacy Policy

Your data security is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect minimal necessary information, including:

  • Contact Information: Name and Email Address (for delivery).
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history and order IDs.
  • Device Data: IP address and device type (for fraud prevention).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to:

  • Process your orders and deliver digital products.
  • Send transactional emails (Receipts, QR Codes).
  • Provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations (KYC/AML).

3. Payment Security

NoorLink does not store your credit card information. All payments are processed securely through certified payment gateways (Stripe/Airwallex) using industry-standard encryption (SSL).

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary data with our telecom partners (e.g., to activate a specific bundle on a network).

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data, contact: support@noorlink.com
